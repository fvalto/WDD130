Hello there! I am Claudia Vergara, for a few years I have been passionate about mental health, as a psychologist, I seek to accompany people's processes and provide them with the tools so that they can get to know themselves and others from a different perspective.

My work has focused mainly on children and adolescents, providing accompaniment and help to people in different age ranges. I have also provided advice to families regarding evolutionary psychodevelopment in the different processes of childhood and adolescence, understanding the particularity of each individual, trying different strategies so that parents or caregivers of our childhood can connect with them and thus strengthen our families.

On this page you can find information and psychoeducational material that can help you understand the different development processes in childhood and adolescence, tips and advice regarding the different ways of educating and providing emotional support to those who depend on us, you can also schedule psychological attention with me is in the modality you prefer, both virtually and in person.
