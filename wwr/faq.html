Frequently Asked Questions

1. What do I need to bring with me to do rafting?

In general, we will provide all the equipment, but you should bring the following:

  • Swimsuit
  • Towel
  • Change of clothes (underwear included)
  • In cold days, bring extra warm clothes
  • Any necessary medication

    3. Can I drink alcohol/eat before the rafting?

    For your safety and that of your companions, you should not drink any alcohol before rafting, after that there's no problem
    Alcoholic people or under the influence of drugs will not be allowed in the activity.
    Eating before rafting is actually recommended it as it is a physical activity that requires energy.

    4. What is the minimum age allowed for rafting?

    As a standard, we require a minimum age of 12 years to participate in the rafting.
    But this depends on any condition, about the person, the weather and the river we were to visit that day.
    If you have any concerns, please contact us by email

    5. Can I go rafting if I am pregnant?

    For your safety and that of your baby we cannot allow pregnant women in the rafting.
